About us?

We are an arts academy with programs that develop the artistic skills of students, focused on their interests to contribute more to their training, in order to boost their creativity and focus. In the academy we have multiple instruments such as guitar, drums, electric guitar, bass and keyboard mainly, however you can bring your own instrument. Likewise, we have excellent teachers and students with a great love for music, as well as being good human beings with a taste for teaching both teachers and students.

Why we?

Because we are not just an academy, it is a home where you can express your emotions through music, a pleasant home with people of different tastes, a home where you can learn at your own pace and without pressure. In addition, we highly value the people who give their commitment, respect, trust and peace in us. So, from our side you will feel those values and likewise from the people around us in the academy.
